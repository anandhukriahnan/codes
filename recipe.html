Delicious Chocolate Chip Cookies

Preparation time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter,softend
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cups Chocolate chips

    • Instructions

    1. Preheat oven to 350 degreesF(175degree C).
    2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until smooth.
    3. Beat in the eggs one at time, then stir in the vanilla.
    4. In a separte bowl,combine the flour and baking soda; gradually add to the butter mixture.
    5. Stir in the Chocolate chips.
    6. Drop by rounded tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheets.
    7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
